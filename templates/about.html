About the Social Network Navigator

The Social Network Navigator lets you view your Facebook friends and the connections between them.

By using a network layout algorithm that tries to bring together connected nodes while keeping unconnected ones apart, you get a visualization of your social network that typically identifies "clusters", i.e. the bigger social groups that your friends can be grouped into, and the connections between them.

While there are several apps which create similar visualizations, the main feature about this app is that it runs completely in your browser. This means that

  • your friendship data is only sent to your browser, not to a remote server, and
  • you can interact with the network, e.g. hover over nodes to see who they are, and click on them to visit their profile right from the app.

You are experiencing a very early version of the Social Network Navigator. It has been tested on a very limited number of networks (as of this writing, exactly one) and I have no idea if it will scale to work on other networks. I'd be happy for you to report any problems you encounter, or even if you don't, to get a screenshot of what it renders for you!

Privacy

  • We do not store any information about you on our servers.
  • In fact, we do not receive any information about you on our servers. All our servers do is provide some HTML and JavaScript that runs in your browser and pulls the data from Facebook onto your machine, where it is rendered.
  • The only data that is stored when you use this app is whatever information Facebook stores (that you use it, when you use it, ...), some of which may be passed on to us for tracking app usage.
  • The Social Network Navigator runs on standard Facebook permissions because it doesn't do anything beyond pulling friendship information; it will ask for the optional permission to post to your stream so in the future, you will be able clicl a button and post on your stream that you're using the app. It will never post to your stream unless you explicitly tell it to!
